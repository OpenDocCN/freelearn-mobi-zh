Adding Auto Layout to the Explore screen's section header

As you have seen in previous chapters, Auto Layout ensures that the UI adapts to the device's screen size and orientation. For example, the Let's Eat app logo on the Launch screen stays in the exact center of the screen regardless of device, and the table view in the Locations screen takes up all of the available screen space even when the device is rotated.
